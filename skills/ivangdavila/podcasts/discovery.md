# Discovery & Recommendations

## Finding New Shows

Based on listening patterns:
- Similar topics to current subscriptions
- Guests that appeared on shows I like
- Recommended by hosts I trust
- Different perspectives on topics I care about

---

## Guest Watchlist

VIP guests to track across all podcasts:
```
## My Guest Watchlist
- Naval Ravikant
- Andrew Huberman
- Tim Ferriss
- etc.

Alert when any appear on ANY podcast, not just subscriptions.
```

---

## Topic Alerts

Track topics across all shows:
- "AI safety discussed"
- "New longevity research"
- Even on shows I don't follow

---

## Avoiding Duplicates

When same guest does podcast tour:
- Identify overlapping content
- Recommend BEST single appearance
- Note unique angles per show
- "Skip X, it's a repeat of Y"

---

## Trending Detection

Surface episodes that:
- Go viral within 24 hours
- Spark social discussion
- Get referenced by other podcasts
- Have unusually high engagement

---

## Backlog Management

When falling behind:
- Identify skippable episodes (outdated, filler, repeat guests)
- Find "catch-up" summary episodes some shows do
- Create bankruptcy plan for massive backlogs
- Suggest starting points to jump back in

---

## Discovery Commands

| User Says | Agent Does |
|-----------|------------|
| "Find podcasts like Huberman" | Suggest similar educational shows |
| "What's trending this week?" | Surface viral/discussed episodes |
| "I'm too far behind on X" | Create catch-up plan |
| "Anything new from [guest]?" | Check all appearances |
